About Me

Database Developer and Technical Writer

Thanks for coming to my website and taking the interest to read a bit about me.

I moved to Seattle, WA from upstate New York about 25 years ago. My life in Seattle has been spent primarily as a developer. My most recent development work was as a Microsoft SQL Server developer for a major telecom company, which I did for 13 years.

In December 2020, I completed my associate’s degree in science. I plan to begin work on a bachelor’s degree in fall 2021 at a new school.

I have enjoyed working with and learning about computers since I was a child and still enjoy taking classes to keep up to date on the latest technology. From most recent to longest ago, the development classes I have taken were in Python, PL/SQL, T-SQL, Java, XML, HTML, C++., C, Visual Basic, Pascal, and BASIC. I treat learning as an ongoing process and take classes every chance I get. My favorite classes in school were always the sciences, especially programming languages, astronomy, and physics.

I am married and have two young children. My family is essential to who I am. For exercise, and as a hobby, I enjoy cycling, which is how I stay healthy.

If you think I’d be a good fit for your team, drop me a line and let’s meet.
